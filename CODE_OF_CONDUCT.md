# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and focused on making housing information easier to understand.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different levels of technical experience.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and kind.
- Avoid sharing personal financial, immigration, or housing details in public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private personal information.
- Spam, scams, or misleading housing claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code of conduct.
